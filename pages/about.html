About Us

Air-conditioning Contractor. Procurement, installation, commissioning of air-conditioning systems, ventilation systems and mechanical building services. Viva was established in 1994 as an air-conditioning contractor. In the last 22 years our expertise has grown and we are able to offer design capabilities as well as operating as a successful HVAC contracting company. We serve commercial, industrial, institutional, and residential customers throughout Eswatini. In 2012 we expanded into retail & wholesale by opening a retail outlet in Matsapha, Eswatini. We also began importing our VIVA brand air-conditioning units as well as offering a wide range of tools, air-conditioning, refrigeration, electrical & hardware products
